Our Mission

Open AI.
For
Everyone.

NeuralChain was built on a single, radical belief: that artificial intelligence — the most powerful technology humanity has ever created — should not be owned and controlled by a handful of corporations.

We are building the infrastructure for a world where AI is open, accessible, and owned by its contributors. Where the value created by intelligence flows back to those who built it.

The Belief

"The same truth that made Bitcoin necessary for finance makes NeuralChain necessary for intelligence. When a handful of corporations control the data, the compute, and the models — the outputs will always serve them first."

Today, the most capable AI systems are owned by a few large companies. Those companies control the training data, the compute, and the outputs. The result is AI that is optimized for engagement, for profit, for control — not for the people who use it.

NeuralChain is different. We've built a permissionless protocol where intelligence is measured, rewarded, and traded openly. Where the best AI wins on merit — not on the size of the company behind it.

We are not building a product. We are building infrastructure. A new layer of the internet where machine intelligence can be produced, evaluated, and exchanged without permission, without censorship, and without a central authority.

The goal is simple: a future where the most powerful AI is not locked behind a paywall or a corporate firewall — but available to anyone, anywhere, as a public resource. Like electricity. Like the internet itself.

History

How we got here.

2016 — 2019
The Idea

Jacob Steeves begins exploring blockchain-based AI coordination. The first Bittensor whitepaper, "BitTensor: An Intermodel Intelligence Measure," introduces the concept of a decentralized market for machine intelligence.

January 2021
Genesis Launch

The network launches as "Kusanagi" — a pure fair launch with no pre-mine, no presale, no founder allocation. Every single TAO has been earned through network participation from day one.

2023 — 2024
Subnet Explosion

The modular subnet system launches. Subnet registrations explode — from one network to 50+ specialized AI markets. The dTAO governance upgrade improves decentralization. Institutional interest grows.

December 2025
First Halving

The first halving event cuts daily emissions from 7,200 to 3,600 TAO. Grayscale files for a Bittensor Trust. Market cap reaches $3.43B. The network enters a new era of programmed scarcity.

2026
NeuralChain Era

The network hosts dozens of active subnets, processes real AI workloads, and continues to attract developers and capital from around the world. The vision of a decentralized AI economy is becoming reality.

The Future
What's Next

EVM compatibility enables liquid staking and TAO lending. Governance continues to decentralize. More halvings compress supply. The network grows toward its ultimate goal: AI as open, public infrastructure.

Our Values

What we stand for.

01
Permissionless

Anyone can join the network as a miner, validator, staker, or subnet creator. No applications, no approvals, no gatekeepers. The network is open to all who want to contribute intelligence.

02
Meritocracy

The best AI wins. Rewards flow to the most useful contributions — not to the richest players or the most connected teams. Quality is the only currency that matters in the NeuralChain economy.

03
Transparency

Everything on the network is auditable and on-chain. Miner scores, validator rankings, emission distributions, governance votes — all publicly visible, permanently recorded, impossible to falsify.

04
Decentralization

No single entity can own, control, or shut down the NeuralChain network. It is owned collectively by its contributors — the miners, validators, and stakers who built it into what it is today.

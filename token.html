Token Utility

TAO does real work in
the network.

⛏️
Mining Rewards

Miners earn TAO proportional to the quality of their AI outputs as scored by validators. The better the output, the higher the reward share from daily emissions.

⚖️
Validator Incentives

Validators earn TAO for accurately scoring miner outputs. Consistent, honest scoring leads to larger reward shares. Collusion and inaccuracy reduce earnings.

🏦
Staking

Stake TAO into subnets to signal which ones deserve greater emissions. The more TAO staked in a subnet, the greater its reward allocation and market value.

🗳️
Governance

TAO holders participate in network governance through the Senate — voting on proposals from the Triumvirate that shape the future of the protocol.

🔗
Subnet Registration

Bond TAO to register a new subnet slot on the network. This creates economic alignment between subnet creators and the long-term health of the ecosystem.

💱
Service Payments

Applications and users pay TAO to access intelligence from subnets — text generation, image synthesis, data queries. TAO is the universal settlement currency.

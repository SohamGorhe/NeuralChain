How Subnets Work

Every subnet is its own
competitive AI market.

01
🏗️
Subnet Creator

Anyone can launch a subnet by bonding TAO. No permission required — just commitment and code. The creator designs the incentive structure for their specific AI task.

02
⛏️
Miners Compete

Miners register on the subnet and begin providing AI outputs. They compete globally on quality, speed, and reliability. Better outputs earn more TAO.

03
⚖️
Validators Score

Validators continuously evaluate miner outputs using the subnet's custom scoring logic. Yuma Consensus aggregates scores into final rankings and distributes TAO rewards.
